# frieghtgstcalc# CalcDeck · Suite

A clean, premium, and highly optimized web-based suite of utility calculators designed for fast calculations. Formatted with a mobile-first layout, immediate theme-color matching, and complete credential/payment-manager autofill prevention.

## 🌟 Key Features

- **GST Calculator**: Calculate base price, GST amount, and final total with standard rate presets or custom percentages.
- **Per Gram Calculator**: Calculate item price per unit (gram, kg, days, or pieces), with custom making charges (percent, per gram, fixed) and desired quantity calculations.
- **Discount Calculator**: Support for percentage/fixed discounts, stacked discounts, BOGO (Buy One Get One) structures, and markup/profit margin analysis.
- **Fuel Cost Calculator**: Determine total fuel cost, fuel required, and price per kilometer based on fuel type (Petrol, Diesel, CNG), average mileage, and distance.
- **Comparison Tool**: Add and compare multiple calculations side-by-side.
- **History System**: Saves recent calculations locally. Click any history entry to prompt and reload its inputs directly back into the calculator.
- **Privacy & Autofill Lock**: Strong defenses blocking password managers and payment overlays from interrupting input fields.

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

- **Core**: HTML5, Vanilla CSS3, ES6 JavaScript.
- **PWA**: Service Worker (`sw.js`) and Manifest (`manifest.json`) for offline caching and native app installation.
